Overview

What is NF Solutions?

NF Solutions is an IT services and digital engineering company helping enterprises modernise their technology platforms. Their services span cloud infrastructure, AI-driven solutions, data analytics, and end-to-end digital transformation.

Their clients are mid-to-large businesses who need trusted technology partners. The design challenge was to communicate technical depth clearly while making the brand feel approachable and credible.

🎯

The Design Challenge

Translate complex IT services into a clean, user-friendly experience that builds trust with enterprise decision-makers and communicates value quickly.

👤

Primary Users

CTOs, IT Managers, and Procurement leads at mid-to-large enterprises evaluating IT service partners.

Before & After

Starting from scratch — the right way.

Unlike a redesign, this project was built from the ground up. That meant defining the visual language, information architecture, and interaction patterns before a single screen was designed.

⬜ Without UX Design
  • ✕No clear information hierarchy — users had to search for services
  • ✕Generic visual style with no brand personality
  • ✕No dedicated service pages to support sales conversations
  • ✕Poor mobile experience for on-the-go stakeholders
  • ✕Lack of trust signals and social proof elements
✦ After UX Design
  • ✓Clear service navigation with logical user journeys
  • ✓Consistent design system — colours, type, spacing, components
  • ✓Dedicated service & solution pages optimised for conversion
  • ✓Fully responsive layouts for all device sizes
  • ✓Strategic placement of case studies and trust elements

Design Process

How I approached the design

01

Discovery & Research

Analysed competitor IT service websites to identify UX patterns, visual benchmarks, and gaps. Reviewed NF Solutions' service offerings to map user goals to business objectives.

Competitor Analysis Business Goals
02

Information Architecture

Created a sitemap defining all pages and their relationships. Designed the navigation structure to support multi-service browsing without overwhelming users.

Sitemap Navigation Design
03

Brand Direction & Design System

Established the visual foundation — selected a professional colour palette with strong accent colours, paired a bold display font with a readable body font, and built reusable components.

Typography Colour System Components
04

Wireframing

Sketched low-fidelity wireframes for all key pages. Focused on layout, content hierarchy, and call-to-action placement before adding visual detail.

Low-fi Wireframes Layout Planning
05

High-Fidelity UI Design

Produced polished, pixel-perfect screens in Figma. Designed the homepage, service pages, about page, contact page, and key landing sections with full visual detail.

Figma Hi-fi Design Responsive
06

Handoff & Collaboration

Prepared developer handoff documentation, annotated specs, and worked alongside the engineering team to ensure accurate implementation of the designs.

Dev Handoff Annotation
Design Decisions

Key design decisions I made

🧭
Service-First Navigation

I prioritised services in the top navigation so enterprise visitors could immediately find what they needed — reducing cognitive load and time-to-action.

Navigation UX B2B Patterns
🏗️
Structured Content Blocks

Each service page followed a consistent structure: problem → solution → benefits → CTA. This pattern made content scannable and decision-making easier.

Content Strategy Conversion UX
💼
Trust-Building Elements

Strategically placed client logos, service certifications, and outcome-led copy to build credibility with cautious enterprise buyers.

Social Proof Enterprise UX
⚡
Visual Hierarchy in Complex Pages

Used type scale, white space, and section markers to guide users through information-heavy service pages without overwhelming them.

Visual Hierarchy Typography

Key Learnings

What this project taught me

🧩

Design systems save time at scale

Building components upfront allowed me to design pages faster and keep visual consistency without repeating work.

🗣️

B2B UX reduces friction

Enterprise buyers scan for proof and clarity. Every design choice needed to answer "why trust this company?" quickly.

📐

Structure before style

Spending more time on IA and wireframes meant fewer revisions later. Good foundations accelerate the entire project.

🔄

Real constraints sharpen creativity

Working within client brand guidelines and content constraints pushed me to find more creative solutions within boundaries.

The designs are live.
