As the in-house UI/UX designer at Verveo Solutions — a global UX and digital engineering company — I was responsible for shaping the brand's entire visual presence on LinkedIn. This wasn't just about making things look good; it was about turning complex technical services into content people actually stop and read.

Verveo offers services across Digital Engineering, Data & AI, Cloud & Infrastructure, and UX/CX Transformation. My job was to design weekly posts that translated each of these into clear, compelling visuals — breaking down topics like cloud migration strategy, business intelligence, and product engineering into formats that resonate with a professional audience.

Beyond service promotion, I also created a full range of company culture content — celebrating team birthdays, work anniversaries, and new joinings, as well as festival posts for occasions like Women's Day and Holi. Each design had to feel warm and human while staying consistent with Verveo's bold, tech-forward visual identity.

The result was a steady, high-quality stream of content — 2 to 3 posts per week — that helped Verveo build credibility, celebrate its people, and grow its presence as a thought leader in the digital engineering space.
